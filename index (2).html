About Us

Orion Infralabs Private Limited is a state-of-the-art civil and infrastructure testing laboratory dedicated to delivering accurate, reliable, and timely material testing solutions. Established to serve India's rapidly growing infrastructure sector, we specialise in mechanical & chemical testing of building materials, metals, and advanced non-destructive testing (NDT) techniques.

Our modern laboratory is equipped with industry-leading instruments and is staffed by highly qualified engineers and technicians. We operate in strict compliance with national and international standards — including IS, ASTM, and BS codes — ensuring every report we deliver meets the highest benchmarks of accuracy and integrity.

NDT Capabilities

Non-Destructive
Testing

Advanced inspection techniques that evaluate material integrity without causing damage.

Ultrasonic Testing

Detects internal flaws and discontinuities using high-frequency sound waves.

Positive Material Identification (PMI)

Spectroscopic identification of metal alloy composition on-site.

Hardness Testing

Measures surface hardness of metals and concrete using standardised indenters.

Magnetic Particle Testing

Reveals surface and near-surface cracks in ferromagnetic components.

Dye Penetrant Testing

Identifies surface-breaking defects on non-porous materials.

Ultrasonic Thickness Measurement

Precise wall-thickness measurement for pipes, plates & vessels.

Rebound Hammer Testing

Non-destructive estimation of in-situ concrete compressive strength.

Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity Testing

Evaluates concrete quality and homogeneity via pulse travel-time analysis.
